## Overview

The AstroStat Summer School 2024 brought together 48 participants from 22 countries for two weeks of intensive lectures and hands-on tutorials in the mountains of South Tyrol.

## Programme

Morning sessions covered theory, while afternoons were devoted to Python-based practicals using real astronomical datasets from missions including *Gaia*, *XMM-Newton*, and the Rubin Observatory's simulated LSST data.

## Highlights

- Live Bayesian model fitting on *Gaia* DR3 parallax data
- MCMC sampling workshops using `emcee` and `PyMC`
- Invited talk: "Neural posterior estimation for gravitational-wave parameter inference"

## Venue

Sexten (Sesto) is a small village in the Dolomites, providing an ideal environment for focused study and networking.
